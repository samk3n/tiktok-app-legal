Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This application ("the App") is a personal, non-commercial tool used solely by its owner. This policy describes how data is handled.

Who uses the App

Only the owner. The App is not distributed, published, or offered to any other user. No user accounts are created.

What data is accessed

Through the official TikTok Display API, and only after the owner authorizes it via TikTok's OAuth login, the App reads:

  • Basic profile information for the authorizing account (display name, avatar, open ID).
  • The list of videos posted by the authorizing account, including video IDs, titles, post timestamps, and aggregate performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares).

The App does not access private messages, followers' personal information, or any data belonging to accounts other than the authorizing account.

How data is stored

All retrieved data, along with the OAuth refresh token, is stored locally on the owner's personal computer. Nothing is uploaded to any server, shared with any third party, sold, or used for advertising.

How data is deleted

The owner can delete the local data files at any time. Authorization can be revoked at any time from TikTok's settings page under "Manage app permissions," which immediately invalidates the App's access.

Children

The App is not directed at children and is not used by anyone other than the adult owner.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
